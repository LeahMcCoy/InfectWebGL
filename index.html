The Plague Game

How to Play

Controls:

  • W - move forward
  • A - rotate 90 degrees left
  • D - rotate 90 degrees right
  • S - rotate 180 degrees

Goal:

  • Infect the entire town before the time runs out
  • Beware the rat catcher! You're not a cat, you only have one life!
  • Upload your high score, arcade style, to compete against friends and the world!
